How should my file be formatted?

One row per purchased line item. The first row must be column headers. Column names are flexible — common synonyms are detected automatically.

ColumnRequired?Accepted namesExample
productRequired product, item, descriptionGround Beef 80/20
spendOne of spend / quantity spend, amount, cost, total, price1240.50
quantityOne of spend / quantity quantity, qty, units200
unitRecommended unit, uom, measurelb
vendorOptional vendor, supplier, distributorUS Foods
dateOptional date, order_date, period, month2026-01-15
  • Provide spend (USD) and/or quantity + unit. With a mass unit (lb, oz, kg, g, ton) we estimate emissions from weight; otherwise we fall back to spend.
  • Non-mass units like case, each, can, block, bag, gal are fine — those rows use the spend-based estimate.
  • A date column unlocks the by-period breakdown in the report.
  • Amounts may include $ and commas (e.g. $1,240.50).
